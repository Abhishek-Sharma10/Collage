6. Banaras Hindu University - [BHU], Varanasi

Banaras Hindu University (BHU), formerly known as Central Hindu College, is a central university in Varanasi, India. Established in 1916, BHU is the largest residential university in Asia and one of the oldest universities with the status of “Institutes of National Importance” by the UGC. It is also one of the 12 institutions from India in BRICS Universities League, a consortium of leading research universities from BRICS countries. BHU comprises 2 campuses, 5 Institutes, 16 Faculties, and 135 departments offering more than 350 courses at UG, PG, and Research levels. With more than 35,000 students and 1800+ faculty members, BHU attracts students from all over the world. The teacher-to-student ratio at BHU is 18:1. BHU has been consistently ranked among the top 10 universities in India by NIRF and it holds NAAC ‘A grade’ accreditation. BHU has also established MOUs with over 40 national and international universities for student-faculty exchange programs, joint research projects, seminars, etc. Emerging as one of the top universities for placements, BHU students have even bagged a salary package of INR 1 crore per annum.


7.Kasturba Medical College - [KMC], Manipal

KMC Manipal Highlights

  • MBBS is the flagship course offered at KMC Manipal.
  • Admission to M.Sc is based on Manipal Entrance Test (MET) conducted by the college.
  • Manipal Entrance Test (MET) fees is INR 400 for M.Sc courses.
  • KMC Manipal also offers admission to Foreign / NRI / NRI Sponsored based on NEET scores.
  • The expected opening and closing ranks for MBBS at KMC Manipal is 51200 – 24330 for general category.
Kasturba Medical College (KMC), Manipal is private medical college in India established in 1953. It is under the aegis of the Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) and is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI). The college offers admission in various undergraduate, postgraduate, and super-speciality courses in the field of Medicine. KMC Manipal accepts merit in NEET UG (MBBS), NEET PG (MD / MS) and NEET SS (DM / MCh). Admission to M.Sc (Medical) courses is through the Manipal Entrance Test (MET) followed by counselling conducted by the college itself. Students are also offered with various scholarships based on merit. As per KMC Manipal NIRF Report 2024, a total of 161 PG (MD / MS / DM / MCh) students were placed in 2023. The median package offered to them stood at INR 15.80 LPA.


8. Jawaharlal Institute of Post Graduate Medical Education and Research - [JIPMER], Pondicherry

Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER) is one of the premier medical institutes in India. JIPMER was established on its current campus in 1964 in Puducherry and is recognized by the Medical Council of India. JIPMER has been ranked as the 5th Best Medical Institute in India as per NIRF Ranking 2024. It has also been recognized as an ‘Institution of National Importance’ by the Government of India. JIPMER Puducherry is among the very few institutions in the country that offer courses from undergraduate to super specialty levels in the field of Medicine and Health Sciences. MBBS is the most popular and opted course at JIPMER Puducherry at the UG level. The institute offers MD/ MS/ MDS at the PG level and DM/ M.Ch at the Super Specialty level. JIPMER accepts national-level entrance exams such as NEET UG, INI CET, and INI SS for admission. B.Sc Nursing, B.Sc, M.Sc Nursing, and MPH are the major paramedical courses offered by JIPMER. JIPMER Campus spreads over an area of 195 Acres. JIPMER also has another campus located in Karaikkal. According to the JIPMER NIRF Report 2023, the median package offered during JIPMER Placements stood at INR 12 LPA for MBBS (UG 5-Year) and INR 24 LPA for PG (3-Year) Batch.


9. Institute of Liver and Biliary Sciences - [ILBS], New Delhi

Institute of Liver and Biliary Sciences (ILBS), is located in Delhi NCR, New Delhi. ILBS was established in 2009 and is an autonomous mono-super speciality hospital for liver and biliary diseases. The university is approved by UGC. According to the NIRF ranking 2024, ILBS ranked in the 34th position among the top medical colleges in India. The courses offered by this institute include PDCC, PGCC, PhD, DM, M.Ch and certificate courses.

  • Admission is based on the Entrance Exam conducted by the institute.
  • ILBS also provides a PG degree in M.Sc Nursing for eligible candidates.
  • The institute accepts both national and international students for admission.
  • Admission to PhD/PDCC/PGCC and certificate courses are offered twice a year.
  • ILBS New Delhi provides students with hostels, a library, a canteen and sports facilities on campus.
ILBS encourages young researchers to choose ILBS as a workplace. The institute offers various sponsorships to benefit the researchers. Some of the sponsorships are as follows:
  • Ramanujan Fellowship from the Science and Engineering Research Board
  • Ramalingaswami fellowship from the Department of Biotechnology
  • VAJRA (Visiting Advanced Joint Research) Faculty Scheme
  • INSPIRE Faculty Fellowship Scheme


10. King George's Medical University - [KGMU], Lucknow

King’s George Medical University (KGMU) is a renowned medical institute located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. The university was founded in 1905 and established in the year 1911. KGMU is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Medical Council of India (MCI). The university offers various UG, PG, Doctoral and PG Diploma courses in various specializations. Admission is offered based on merit and seat availability.

  • KGMU accepts qualifying examinations such as NEET UG, NEET PG, NEET MDS and NEET SS.
  • MBBS and BDS are the most popular courses offered at King George’s Medical University.
  • The university also conducts entrance examinations for certain UG and PG courses.
  • KGMU also offers digital lectures on various topics and students can access them online.
  • The placement cell at KGMU guarantees placement and internship opportunities for final-year students.
  • The Research cell is fully equipped and is funded by the UP government.
  • KGMU offers facilities such as a library, cafeteria, laboratory, hostel and sports facilities.
